Cute Animal Facts

Duck

Ducks have waterproof feathers that help them dry while swimming!


Panda

Pandas spend around 12 hours every day eating bamboo.


Otter

Sea otters hold hands while sleeping so they don't drift apart.

Fox

Foxes can hear tiny animals moving under the snow!


Rabbit

Rabbits can jump nearly three feet high in one hop.


Penguin

Penguins propose to their mates by giving them a special pebble.
